Fun facts about corn

  • Corn is produced on every continent but Antartica
  • The United States produces about 40% of the world's corn
  • It is also the most produced crop in the US
  • One bushel of corn contains about 72,800 kernels and weighs 56 pounds.
  • Many people in the US consider corn to be a vegetable, but it more acurately classified as a grain
  • One of corn's main uses ais as a sweetener; one bushel of corn can sweeten hundreds of cans of soda
  • Europe was without corn before Columbus. When he returned from the new world, he brought back corn
  • On average, a cob with about 800 kernels in 16 rows
  • Thanks to new technology, corn yields per acre have increased roughly five fold
  • Corn is called Maize in many other countries
  • Corn was first grown in South and Central America thousand of years ago
  • Native Americans taught European Settlers how to grow corn
